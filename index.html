How to Play:

Press 'space' to fly
Press 'M' to return to the menu
Press to give direction to your character
Defeat sith lords in lightsaber joust battles and earn points by picking up their lightsabers. Hit them in the back, or aim to be higher on a face-to-face clash. If you don't up lightsabers in time, sith lords will respawn and soon you will be overwhelmed.
